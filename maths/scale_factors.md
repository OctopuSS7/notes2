# Scale Factors

**Example 1:** What do I multiply 5 by to get 11?

$$\begin{align*} 
5x &= 11 &\\ 
x  &= 11 \div 5 &\\ 
   &= \frac{11}{5} &\\  
\end{align*}$$

**Example 2:** What is the scale factor from $$\frac{2}{3}$$ to $$\frac{5}{11}$$

$$\begin{align*} 
\frac{2}{3}x &= \frac{5}{11} &\\ 
x  &= \frac{5}{11} \div \frac{2}{3} &\\ 
   &= \frac{5}{11} \times \frac{2}{3} &\\
   &= \frac{15}{22}  
\end{align*}$$
